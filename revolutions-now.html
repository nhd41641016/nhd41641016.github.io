                  —Kirthi Jayakumar


                  Map of Arab Spring



                  Arab Spring protests and demonstrations started in Tunisia and quickly spread to much of the Arab world. The protests used social media and live video streaming to spread their message. The Internet allowed protesters to exchange information quickly, helping them to make collective decisions, discuss ideas across the globe, and document news as it was happening.



                  Occupy Wall Street started as a peaceful occupation of Wall Street to denounce the growing income and wealth disparity in the US between the wealthy 1% and the rest of the population. Just like Arab Spring, Occupy Wall Street used social media such as Facebook and Twitter to broadcast their message.
